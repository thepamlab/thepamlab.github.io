Congratulations to PhD student Meixian (Vicky) Li on her acceptance to the upcoming <a href="https://site.hanyang.ac.kr/web/hisphoncog" target="_blank">Hanyang International Symposium on Phonetics and Cognitive Sciences of Language</a> (HISPhonCog 2026) in Seoul! 

She will be giving a talk (co-authored with Profs. Yao and Chang) entitled "Gender effects in creaky voice production in English and Mandarin: Men are creakier".
