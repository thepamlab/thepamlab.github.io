PhD students Jupitara Ray and Meixian (Vicky) Li are both presenting posters at the upcoming <a href="https://labphon.org/labphon20/home" target="_blank">20th Annual Conference on Laboratory Phonology</a> (LabPhon 20) in Montreal! 

The title of Jupitara's presentation (co-authored with Prof. Chang) is "Perception and production change out of sync: A study of Indian English bilinguals".

The title of Vicky's presentation (co-authored with Profs. Yao and Chang) is "Cross-language variation in sociophonetic perception reflects language effects".

Congratulations to all!
