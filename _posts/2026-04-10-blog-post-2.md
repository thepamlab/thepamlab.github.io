Congratulations to Prof. Chang on being awarded the <a href="https://www.polyu.edu.hk/fh/chao-prize/?lang=en" target="_blank">Yuen Ren Chao Prize in Language Science</a> (Early Career Contribution Award) by the Faculty of Humanities at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU)! The Chao Prize is "an international award that honours scholars and researchers who have made distinguished contributions to language science".

He will receive the prize in an awards ceremony during PolyU's International Symposium on Language Science, and will also deliver a Chao Prize Lecture entitled "Language users as chameleons".</p>
